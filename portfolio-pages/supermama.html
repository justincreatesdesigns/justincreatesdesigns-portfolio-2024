SUPERMAMA

Positioning SUPERMAMA as a brand that narrates Singapore's Story. Goals for this project was to drive awareness and engage with the younger demographic based of primary and secondary research. Utilising SEO, Brand Positioning and Content Strategy, we created EDM's, Social Media Posts, Blog Posts and Storyboards for our campaign.
