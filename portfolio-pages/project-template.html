Branding for EcoMart

Developed a cohesive brand identity for EcoMart, a fictional sustainable grocery store, focusing on eco-friendly products and community engagement.

Type of Work: Personal Project

Skills Applied: Branding, Logo Design, Marketing Collateral

Year: 2023

Collaborators: Solo Project

GO BACK TO PORTFOLIO

Project Context

This project aimed to explore sustainable branding concepts and develop a visual identity that resonates with environmentally conscious consumers.

Challenges Faced

One challenge was creating a logo that was both modern and reflective of sustainable practices. Feedback from peers helped refine the color palette to enhance the brand's eco-friendly message.

Design Process

The design process included initial sketches, digital mockups, and multiple iterations based on feedback. I utilized Adobe Illustrator to create the final designs.

Skills Developed

  • Proficiency in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.
  • Understanding of branding principles and audience targeting.
  • Collaboration skills through design critiques and peer feedback.
  • Time management and project planning for design projects.

Impact and Reflection

This project significantly enhanced my understanding of branding and its importance in connecting with consumers. The positive feedback from peers reinforced my design choices and motivated me to pursue further studies in design.
