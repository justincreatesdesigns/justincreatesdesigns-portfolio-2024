#StyleItYourWay Campaign for adidas

Developed a content campaign strategy for the adidas Gazelles. Timeless, Empowering & Stylish.

Type of Work: Group Project

Skills Applied: Marketing Collaterals, Campaign Strategy

Year: 2024

Collaborators: Kong Jit Hong, Darren , Rifdi, Pearlyn

GO BACK TO PORTFOLIO

Project Context

This project aimed to explore content marketing concepts and develop a campaign strategy that drive awareness of the adidas Gazelles amongst Gen Z's using content marketing, brand storytelling, and content creation techniques learned in class. Positioning the shoe as a timeless piece, a shoe that will not fall out of trend cycle.

Challenges Faced

One challenge we faced was aligning and creating collaterals with our campaign message which is "A timeless classic that empowers people to feel like a star." We looked backed at our message, research and user persona and decided that we needed something that is catchy and easy to remember hence we came up with #StyleItYourWay. Feedback from my groupmates and our lecturer helped refine the direction of our campaign to enhance the timeless, empowering and stylish message.

Design Process

The design process included moodboards, digital mockups, and multiple iterations based on feedback. I utilized Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop to create the final designs.

Skills Developed

  • Proficiency in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.
  • Understanding of concepts such as the consumer journey, campaign strategy and primary research.
  • Collaboration skills through design critiques and peer feedback.
  • Time management and project planning for campaign projects.

Impact and Reflection

This project significantly enhanced my understanding of campaign strategies and its importance in connecting with consumers. The positive feedback from peers and lecturers reinforced my design choices and motivated me to pursue further studies in design.
