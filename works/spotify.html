Design Brief

Members Involved: Me (Justin), Kong, Lutfiah.

To write, direct and produce a short promotional video about a product. We were given the choice to choose an existing product or make up our own! So we chose Spotify.

The Plan

Before settling on Spotify, we brainstormed to strategize on workload distribution and chose a brand that resonated with all team members. Our approach focused on leveraging motion graphics over traditional outdoor filming and shooting.

We also decided to synchronize our video with a soundtrack that complements the animation and enhances its impact.This decision ensures that our promotional video for Spotify not only engages visually but also resonates audibly, creating a cohesive and compelling viewer experience.

Objectives

  • Develop a moodboard centered around motion graphics.
  • Maintain consistency with Spotify's branding throughout the video.
  • Write a compelling script.
  • Create a detailed storyboard.
  • Execute After Effects (AE) animations.
  • Produce the final video.

Script

After discussion, we divided the video into three sections: the introduction handled by Me (Justin), the phone section by Kong, and the final part by Lutfiah.

To jumpstart our scriptwriting process, we utilized ChatGPT with prompts to generate initial ideas, which proved invaluable in kickstarting our project momentum. You can view the AI-generated script on our Miro Board.

Storyboard

Started production and came up with the storyboard for our video! Each team member contributed to ensuring a seamless flow and consistent visual narrative across all shots.

After completeting our parts we will piece it all up according to the storyboard.

Final

After completeting our objectives, consultation, animating and final touch ups. We have created our end product which is the video above!
