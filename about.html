Hey there,

I’m Justin Jay a “Narrative-Driven” Designer based in Singapore dedicated to crafting designs that tell compelling stories. My journey into graphic design has always been intertwined with a passion for storytelling through visual narratives, I strive to create designs that not only look great but also communicate a powerful message.


My background in my Diploma of Digital Communications has helped me develop a keen sense of how to weave stories into visual form.


I believe that every design project should have a story behind it. Understanding the core and emotional value for each project, allows me to craft designs that engage viewers on a deeper level. My process involves delving into the research and finding narratives to build on and create a visual solution that is not only aesthetically pleasing but also meaningful.


I’m skilled in Adobe Creative Suite, Figma and storytelling techniques that enhance visual communication.



In my spare time, I enjoy exploring different forms of design, whether through movies, books, games and interactive media. This exploration continually inspires my work and fuels my passion for creating designs with a strong narrative focus.


I always look forward to projects where I can utilize my skills and knowledge to bring stories to life through design.
