GO BACK TO PORTFOLIO

Working closely with my team: Kong & Lutfiah my classmates from Ngee Ann, we were tasked to create a promotional video for a product of our choosing. Utilising our new knowledge with AE (After Effects) & creative design process to come up with the video.

Our 3 goals were:

- Finding a style for the target audience?
- How are we gonna show the features?
- Give them a reason to why they should use Spotify.

Getting some inspiration from other Spotify Ad's from youtube. To guage how we want to position our style and vibe.

Script was broken down into 3 parts.

Part 1 of the script is to highlight that Spotify is a platform that is accessible anywhere.

Part 2 is to highlight the infinite number of genres there are on the platform.

Finally, part 3 is the combination of these last 2 parts the reason why people should use Spotify. Key messaging was that you can find any song, anywhere in the world at anytime.

Some of the things we also took into account was the breakdown of how the workload should be split and how do we piece it all together. As the script was already broken down into 3 parts, we decided that we would all just take 1 part and animate as according to the storyboard.

Storyboarding! Discussing with my team and finding the right motion to convey the message from the script.
