Working with my group mates Kong and Darren. Using competitor analysis to position the brand. We created Ripple, a brand that loves "Making Waves" by becoming a hub of advocacy for ethical and sustainable brands.

Noun List, Sketching and Vectorizing was the process for the logo. Using words that relate to the brand like sustainability and community to form the final Logo Mark.
