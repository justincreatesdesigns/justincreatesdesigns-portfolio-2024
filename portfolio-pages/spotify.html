Spotify Video Ad

A motion graphics-driven promotional video showcasing the features of using Spotify.

PROJECT BRIEF

Write, direct and produce a short promotional video about a product, you can choose an existing product or make up your own.

SERVICES

Motion Graphics

YEAR

2023

Working closely with my team: Kong & Lutfiah my classmates from Ngee Ann, we were tasked to create a promotional video for a brand of our choosing. Utilising our new knowledge with AE (After Effects) & creative design process we were able to create the video. Below is our workflow.

Our 3 goals were:

- Finding a style for the target audience?
- How are we gonna show the features?
- Give them a reason to why they should use Spotify.

At that point we were also gathering assets and music that we can use for the video.

Getting some inspiration from other Spotify Ad's from youtube. To guage how we want to position our style and vibe.

Exercising some script writing. Breaking them into 3 parts.

Storyboarding!

And finally execution!
