Singapore Garden Festival

Graphics, Colours and Generative AI. To create the appeal of fun around the event I tapped onto humour as part of the design. Using funny phrases and graphics that relate to the activities for the event.

PROJECT BRIEF

This group assignment serves to apply your creative design process, digital imaging skills, visual design principles and understanding of web graphics for this module. Students are to work in pairs to produce two deliverables for the National Parks Board.

GO BACK TO PORTFOLIO

Original version of the project. Started off as a group assignment where I collaborated with Kong to come up with the concept and style that we wanted to approach.

After sometime I decided to redesign the old posts utilising my newly learned knowledge from my course.

For Typography I initially went with a serif typeface to accentuate the vintage vibe but eventually for legibility I chose a more sleek and simple typeface that also pairs well with the illustrations. The switch to illustrations was to bring out the direction I was going for based on the moodboard and keeping the humour aspect.
