People Watching

The wonders of life and people. Maybe cats too.

As humans we are the most complex creatures to exist in the animal kingdom, animals will look at us and think we are crazy. We walk on two feet, eat all kinds of meat, experiment on plants, eat them too and ride on metal machines, plus many other things humans can do today. Putting us on the opposite spectrum of how animals live.

In comparison, for example, cats. Generally, the things they do are eat, poop, run around and maybe if they get tired they sleep for half the day until someone disturbs their peace then meows at people for more food or attention then sleep again. Their lives tend to be more simple and consistent. Unlike us, our daily lives can vary from the day you start waking up or even before you sleep. Our patterns are unpredictable and endless to the point that there are even applications to help you manage your time and try to do the predicting for you.

But just like cats we also like attention. We do that by partaking in activities such as playing video games, hanging out with friends, posting on social media, and jumping out of planes. OK, the last one is a bit extreme but you get the point.

People are amazing creatures capable of doing many things. We display a wide range of emotions, thoughts and behaviours and our uniqueness comes from individual experiences, upbringings, cultures and genetics.

So, embrace your differences. Everyone around you is unique too.
