Scoop EDM Design

The full process for my Scoop EDM!

School Project

EDM

Graphic Design

Overview Objective

Scoop Wholefoods (https://sg.scoopwholefoods.com) sends out a discount coupon on a member's birthday month and would like you to create a new EDM for this purpose.

Challenge

Some issues I faced when creating the design for this EDM was getting a cohesive layout, choosing the right typeface as well as a to create compelling graphic with the assets given to me.

How can I capture the attention of the user to open the email, get them hooked onto reading the email and eventually claiming their reward and stick to Scoops identity of being a very eco-friendly by using recyclable materials.

Solution

Used a moodboard to get the style and layout that I want.

Started mindmapping to get ideas of what to use for graphics and vibe for the EDM.

Before I went to designing the EDM I drafted out some layouts using shapes to visualize that final design.

Design

Designing the EDM was a rocky journey, working with my friends and colleagues for feedback. The EDM's design has been reiterated to what it is now. (Wish I still had the old versions to show but yea they were something...)
